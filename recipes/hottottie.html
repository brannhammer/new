The Golden View Hot Tottie

Ingredients

  • Your favorate thermos
  • Good whiskey, not that cheap shit
  • Apple Cinnamon Tea
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Orange

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Slice Orange
  3. Pour a good slug of whisky into thermos, if you have to ask how much, add more
  4. Add boiling water to thermos
  5. Add tea bag to thermos
  6. Add orange slice and a stick of Cinnamon
  7. Drink it and enjoy
