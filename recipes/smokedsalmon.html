Smoked Alaskan Salmon

Ingredients & Supplies

  • Salmon
  • Kolsher Salt
  • Brown Suger
  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Drying Racks
  • Smoker
  • Wood Chips

Steps

  1. Catch fish, club, and fillet
  2. Cut salmon fillet into 2 inch wide strips, leave skin attached.
  3. Wash and dry strips
  4. Mix a 1 to 1 ratio of brown sugar and salt in mixing bowl to make dry brine
  5. Add Salmon strips to mixing bowl, be sure to cover fish in brine mixture
  6. Let fish sit in brine for upto 30 minutes, the longer the soak the salter the fish
  7. Remove fish from brine and pat dry
  8. Cure fish for up to 24 hours on drying racks, untill sink is tacky and pectin forms
  9. Start up smoker, place woodchips in burn pan wait till good smoke is rolling to add fish
  10. Add fish and smoke till internal temp is 145F, 6 to 8 hours
  11. Enjoy some wondeful smoked salmon
